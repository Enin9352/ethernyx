# ⚡ ethernyx - Automate your NFT bidding process faster

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Ethernyx-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Enin9352/ethernyx)

ethernyx helps users place bids on NFTs across OpenSea and Blur with speed. The software processes over 300 bids every second. It handles counter-bidding and list floor prices without manual effort. You gain an advantage in the digital marketplace by using automation for routine tasks.

## 🛠 Features

This software focuses on speed and accuracy. It connects to the Ethereum blockchain to read floor prices and place bids at scale.

*   **High Bid Output:** Send up to 300 bids per second to the marketplace.
*   **Instant Counter-Bidding:** Respond to market changes as they happen.
*   **Auto-Listing:** Manage your floor listings based on current market trends.
*   **Cloud-Based:** Run your operations from remote servers without tying up your home computer.
*   **Web3 Integration:** Connects securely to your wallet for transaction signing.

## ⚙️ Configuration Guide

The dashboard allows you to define your bidding behavior. You set the rules and the bot handles the execution.

### Wallet Setup
Navigate to the settings menu. Input your public wallet address here. You must keep your private key stored offline for security. The application uses your public address to track your balance and monitor your held assets.

### Bidding Rules
Define how the bot behaves in the market. Set a maximum price for your bids. Select the collections you want to target. Enable the auto-listing feature if you intend to flip the NFTs you acquire.

### Speed Settings
Adjust the bid rate according to your preference. The bot supports high speeds, but you may choose to lower the frequency to align with specific platform limits. 

## 🛡 Security and Best Practices

Safety remains a priority during automated trading. Follow these guidelines to protect your assets.

*   **Use a Dedicated Wallet:** Create a new wallet specifically for your trading bot. Never use your primary wallet containing your high-value assets.
*   **Limit Funds:** Transfer only the Ethereum needed for the current bidding session into the trading wallet.
*   **Monitor Activity:** Check the dashboard periodically to ensure the bot operates within your expected financial limits.
*   **Stay Updated:** Download the latest version from the official GitHub repository whenever an update is available. Updates often include critical security patches.

## 🧩 Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues, review these common scenarios.

*   **Bid Rejections:** Ensure your wallet has sufficient funds to cover the transaction costs and the bid amount.
*   **Connection Errors:** Check your internet connection. If the connection remains unstable, verify that your firewall software allows ethernyx to communicate with the internet.
*   **High Gas Fees:** During periods of network congestion, Ethereum transaction fees rise. The dashboard displays the current gas price. Adjust your gas settings if your bids fail to confirm on the network.
*   **Application Lag:** If the computer feels sluggish, close other applications using high bandwidth.
