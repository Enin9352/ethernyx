# Ethernyx FAQ — NFT Bidding Bot for OpenSea & Blur

Answers to the most common questions about the **Ethernyx NFT bidding bot**. Start at **[app.ethernyx.io](https://app.ethernyx.io)**.

### What is Ethernyx?
Ethernyx is the **fastest cloud-based NFT bidding bot** for **OpenSea** and **Blur**. It automates collection, trait, and token bidding, instantly counter-bids when you're outbid, auto-cancels stale offers, and auto-lists your NFTs with a built-in Floor Lister.

### Which marketplaces does it support?
**OpenSea** and **Blur** — both from a single dashboard.

### What is the fastest NFT bidding bot?
Ethernyx places **up to 300+ token bids per second**, roughly **3× faster** than typical competitors.

### Do I need to download or install anything?
No. Ethernyx is **100% cloud-based** and runs in your browser on **any device**, including mobile. No VPS, no local node.

### How do I sign in?
With your **Web3 wallet** (MetaMask, WalletConnect, and more). No passwords — you authenticate by signing with your wallet.

### Can it automatically list or sell my NFTs?
Yes. The **Floor Lister** automatically lists your NFTs at the floor (or your target price), keeps the price updated, and **instantly re-lists** after a bid fills.

### What bidding types are supported?
- **Collection bidding** — offers across an entire collection.
- **Trait bidding** — offers only on items with specific traits.
- **Token / item bidding** — offers on specific token IDs (Starter plan and above).

### Is my private key safe?
Yes. Keys are **encrypted at rest** and isolated in a **hardened private service** with IP allow-listing. They **never leave that service** and are **never written to logs**.

### How much money do I need to start?
From **~0.2 ETH** to explore safely; **1 ETH or more** to trade for real numbers.

### How much time does it take to set up?
A couple of hours to dial in your first tasks and understand each strategy. After that it **runs in the cloud** — you only check in to tweak.
