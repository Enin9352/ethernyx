## What the limits mean

- **Tasks** — how many bidding / listing strategies you can run at once. Every paid plan is **unlimited** except Lite.
- **Bids per second** — your maximum bid throughput. Higher tiers win faster-moving collections.
- **Wallets** — how many wallets you can connect under one account.
- **Item (token) bidding** — bid on specific token IDs / traits, not just whole collections (Starter and above).

## How payment works

- Pay directly in **ETH** on Ethereum.
- Choose **monthly** or **annual** billing (annual saves up to **45%**).
- Every payment is **validated on-chain** before your plan activates.
- The **Individual** plan offers custom limits for power users and teams — ask in [Telegram](https://t.me/ethernyx_tools).

## How much capital do I need?

- From **~0.2 ETH** on your balance you can see how everything works without serious risk.
- To trade for real numbers, plan for **1 ETH or more**.
